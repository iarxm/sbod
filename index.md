## About

This site provides the organisation for strength training related to high performance surfing

A. Train to train
B. Train to compete
C. Compete to train

The goal is to establish the entire practise of physical preperation necessary for high performance technical and competitive progression

The secondary goal is to do so in a way which produces world class athlleticism not just in physicality but in maturity and training independence
