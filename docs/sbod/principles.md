# Guide 

[[toc]]

## Principles

- A. Train to train
- B. Train to compete
- C. Compete to train

The goal is to establish the entire practise of physical preparation necessary for high performance technical and competitive progression

The secondary goal is to do so in a way which produces world class athleticism not just in physicality but in maturity and training independence


#### Principles Of Training

Think in four year cycles


#### Training Types

- Strength forms
- Flexibility
- Stabilisation
- Weight training
    - Heavy rock lifting
    - Kettle Dunes
- Very high intensity
- Cross training
- Paddle technique / strength
